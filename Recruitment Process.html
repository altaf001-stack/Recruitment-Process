Recruitment Process

Lead Generation

  • Ads are created by the marketing team and uploaded to portals.
  • Leads are sourced from job portals, Facebook, car stickers, Uber VMP, Google Ads, and social media.
  • Centralized data is shared with the calling team for outbound calls.
  • Leads are assigned to city-specific calling campaigns using the Ameyo application.

Tele Calling

Roles and responsibilities of a telecaller include:

  • Interacting with calls, addressing inquiries, and ensuring customer satisfaction.
  • Following organizational guidelines and promoting services to potential customers.
  • Maintaining accurate records of all interactions in Ameyo or spreadsheets.

Dispositions

  • Interested: Pilot shows interest and proceeds to the next steps.
  • Not Interested: Pilot explicitly declines the service.
  • Call Back: Pilot requests a follow-up at a later time.

On-Boarding

Steps in the onboarding process:

  1. Appointment scheduling and follow-up by the telecaller.
  2. Document verification upon the Pilot's arrival.
  3. Enrollment in the Everest application and Uber ID creation.
  4. Background verification and driving test.
  5. Training sessions covering company policies, safe driving practices, and other topics.
  6. Deposit payment and assignment of a Relationship Manager.

Vehicle Allocation Process

  • Verification of deposit and allocation readiness in the Hawkeye system.
  • Vehicle inspection and approval by the Pilot.
  • Final allocation and handover after all checks are complete.
